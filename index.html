The Dictionary

Chutzpah
[ Chootz-pah ] Noun
Brutal honesty, usually at the price of sounding presumptuous, arrogant or rude.
“It takes chutzpah to tell your boss he’s wrong.”
Balagan
[ Ba-la-gan ] Noun
Organized chaos.
“Life would be very boring without a pinch of balagan.”
Yihiye Beseder
[ Yi-hi-ye Be-se-der ] Phrase
Everything will be just fine. A critical mindset for entrepreneurs.
“Things may look bad now, but yihiye beseder.”
Firgun
[ Feer-goon ] Noun
Being truly supportive of and happy for someone.
“I would never be here if it weren’t for your firgun, thank you.”
Yalla
[ Ya-llah ] Phrase
Let’s go! Hurry up! 
“Yalla, the show’s about to start!”
Leezrom
[ Leez-rohm ] Verb
Welcoming the unexpected.
“Life is all about leezrom. Seize an opportunity and see where it takes you.”
Combina
[ com-bee-na ] Noun
An unconventional solution to a problem.
“Unfortunately, there’s no combina when it comes to tax law.”
Dugri
[ Doo-gree ] Adjective
Listen up, here comes the punch. PS, you’re not going to like it.
“Dugri, I think you’re making a huge mistake.”
Tachles
[ Tach-less ] Noun
The bottom line of a long discussion.
“Tachles, it all comes down to money”
